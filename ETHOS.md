# Ethos — Ribbon

Ribbon is a web-based analog ribbon synthesizer — an instrument that lives in the browser, inspired by hardware like the Korg Monotribe. It's for making sound, not for showing off features.

## Guiding Principles

- **It's an instrument, not an app.** Ribbon should feel like picking up a physical thing and playing it. Immediacy matters. The gap between intent and sound should be as small as possible.
- **Expressiveness over complexity.** A ribbon controller is inherently expressive — continuous pitch, pressure, velocity. Lean into that. Don't bury the player in menus and options when a single gesture should suffice.
- **Accessible synthesis.** You shouldn't need to understand signal routing to make something that sounds good. But the depth should be there for those who want it.
- **Play anywhere.** Browser-native, mobile and desktop. If you have a screen and a speaker, you can play.
